Our Mission

Due to our expanding reliance on computer technology, computer science literacy has become just as important as any other core subject taught in schools, and yet it is often overlooked. Just like writing or basic arithmetic, programming is now a fundemental skill every person should have a basic understanding of. Our goal is to fill this vital gap in childhood education with a personalized curriculum for each student to create an engaging learning experience driven by each students invidividual interests.
